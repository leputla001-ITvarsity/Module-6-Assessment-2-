Audi RS7

The Audi S7 Sportback is powered by a sporty petrol engine. With 331 kW of power and a torque of 600 Nm, the 2.9 V6 TFSI (Fuel consumption combined: 11.0–6.4 l/100 km; CO₂ emissions combined: 197 g/km) accelerates the vehicle from 0 to 100 km/h in 4.6 seconds. The standard quattro all-wheel drive transfers the power to the road. An optional sport differential on the rear axle improves the vehicle’s positioning when cornering.
